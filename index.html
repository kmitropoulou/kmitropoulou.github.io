About

I am a compiler developer at Intel, Santa Clara. I am working on LLVM development.
I was a post-doctoral researcher in the Computer Architecture Group, Computer Laboratory, University of Cambridge and I was working with Dr Timothy M. Jones.
I did my Ph.D. in CArD - Compiler and Architecture Design Group in the School of Informatics at the University of Edinburgh under the supervision of prof. Marcelo Cintra.

Research Interests

My research is on compiler-based error detection methodologies and focus on making them faster without decreasing reliability. Among others, I am interested in:
  • compilers
  • fault-tolerance
  • computer architecture
